What is the difference between your case options?
We know that people like to protect their phones in different ways, so we offer two options. The first one, Snap, is primarily cosmetic. As the name suggests, it's a snap-on case that mainly serves to protect the back of your phone, but not to be too bulky in the process, and it is an extension of the shape of your phone. You bought that shape, so we won’t make a mockery out of its curves. We only raise the bezels around the camera a bit, so your camera is extra protected. But don’t worry, the front also has a lip that protects your screen, should you accidentally drop it face down. The second option is a much tougher version, with more material being used to cover the phone. It sports a thicker lip on the front, with chamfered corners, so you can still reach everything on your screen. Additionally, a silver-based component is added to make the exterior antimicrobial, so you can sport this truly tough case anywhere and everywhere, and not worry about bacteria building up.

We know waiting can be hard, but if something doesn’t seem right, every order comes with a tracking number you can check yourself. Just check your purchase e-mail/receipt. If something still doesn’t seem quite right, don’t hesitate to contact our customer support.
What are your cases made from?
Our cases are made from wheat straw, a by-product from harvesting wheat grain. This is beneficial, as unlike traditional plastics, used in most phone cases, wheat straw doesn’t require the usage of fossil fuels. Wheat straw is also incredibly durable, and is a renewable and sustainable way of producing long lasting phone cases for our customers.
Do you only make cases for iPhones?
The short answer is yes. The long answer is that with so many different options for Android phones, it’s difficult to produce something for every major brand's flagship product, and as they tend to change their shapes every iteration as well, setting up tooling through our production partners would be extremely cumbersome and expensive, especially as we produce in such limited quantities at a time. With the iPhone, we can often reuse molds, and several of their models have essentially interchangeable covers. We aren’t excluding that it could become an option in the future, but at the moment, iPhone only.
I really liked a previous collection, will you make more?
We want our products to be unique and limited, and we believe old customers might not be too happy, if their unique product certainly entered production again. That being said, sequels are a thing, and if we see an idea that makes sense, you can be sure we will be trying it out.
